While I’ve had a lot of jobs in my career, one thing has always remained consistent - the involvement of technology. From being a Brewer to a Software Support Specialist, technology has always been integrated into my day to day responsibilities. With over 6 years in the IT Support industry, I was tired of supporting software that was consistently crashing, had bugs, or just didn’t work. So, I decided to take matters into my own hands and join the Software Engineer Immersive Bootcamp at General Assembly.

My goal is to create software that is consistent, effective, and has the client and user experience in mind.

A lifelong Georgia resident, I am a big fan of Atlanta sports (Atlanta United, Braves, and Falcons). Staying true to my Georgia roots, I also love to smoke meat on my offset smoker, which is paired best with my homebrewed beer. I enjoy visitng new breweries in the Atlanta area, adding a new movie to my collection, or hanging out with my wife and dog.
